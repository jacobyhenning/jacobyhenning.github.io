About Me

The person behind
the models.

A driven professional with a background in Applied Mathematics, Jacoby combines analytical precision with the grit of a Pittsburgh heritage that treats hard work as a family legacy. This "steel in the blood" mentality, combined with growing up in the East Bay, forged a grounded, community-first perspective that shapes how he approaches every problem — with curiosity, grit, and a deep sense of purpose.

That same competitive drive fueled a diverse athletic career — playing collegiate rugby and earning a spot as a true walk-on for a Division I baseball program.

Beyond the field, Jacoby transitioned this competitive energy into the coaching ranks of Division I Women's Basketball, where a personable leadership style met an uncompromising standard for excellence.

Whether optimizing complex datasets or managing high-stakes athletic operations, Jacoby remains a dedicated perfectionist who thrives at the intersection of technical strategy and team-oriented execution.
